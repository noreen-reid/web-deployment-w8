How to Build the Perfect Skincare Routine

How to Build the Perfect Skincare Routine

  • Noreen Team
  • May 14, 2025
  • 8 Comments

A consistent skincare routine is the foundation of healthy, glowing skin. At Noreen Cosmetics, we believe in simplicity backed by science. Whether you're a beginner or a skincare enthusiast, here's how to build a routine that works.

First, identify your skin type: oily, dry, combination, or sensitive. This will guide your product choices. Always begin with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and excess oil.

"Your skin is an investment, not an expense. Treat it with care, and it will glow for years to come."

After cleansing, use a toner to balance your skin’s pH, followed by a serum that targets your specific concerns (e.g., Vitamin C for brightening or hyaluronic acid for hydration). Then apply moisturizer to lock in moisture and protect the skin barrier.

Don't Forget Sunscreen

Sunscreen is your best defense against premature aging and skin damage. Apply it every morning, even on cloudy days. At Noreen, we recommend our lightweight SPF 50+ cream that sits perfectly under makeup.

Weekly Treatments

Exfoliate 1–2 times a week using a gentle scrub or AHA/BHA exfoliant. Masks (like our Rose Glow Clay Mask) are great for a spa-like treat and added skin benefits.
